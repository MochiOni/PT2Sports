Intramural are recreational activity inside a facility to encourage school-wide involvement of all students in physical
activity. Goal of Intramural Sports are to make fun, social and respectful atmosphere and promoting sporting behavior
among all the participants.


In our school past intramural, each strand fight for gold medal, silver and bronze.
From HE, they earned six(6) gold medal, two(2) silver and two(2) bronze medal.
In ICT, we earned two(2) gold medals, one(1) silver and four(4) bronze.
And in GAS, they earned two(2) gold medals, nine(9) silver and four(4) medals.
Lastly in HUMSS, they earned six(6) gold medals, three(3) silver and two(2) bronze medals.
